Our project

For this project, our team decided to tackle a type of game that is sometimes abandoned and remaster it as a multiplayer game in order to create a game between horror and adventure: “Mary, where are you? ".
This game consists of collecting clues in order to find Mary's murderer and her child, both taken from the famous Bloody Mary myth. A little reminder, this myth tells the story of a woman in the 20th century who allegedly lost her child during a miscarriage and who was locked alive in a coffin by her father with the only escape being a wire connected to a bell if she never regained consciousness, but the bell never rang.
Our goal is to give you the biggest scare of your life. During the creation of this game, our team made up of Wissame, Daphné, Théo and Yness had to get out of their comfort zone in order to learn and discover 3D modeling (Blender), the creation of a multi-player system (4 characters), sound development (Music Maker) and many more. It is thanks to this learning that the first forms of the game appear and motivate to continue to move forward. Indeed, the decor and the atmosphere go hand in hand to give way to an image of the game that becomes clearer over the weeks.
However, there is still a long way to go before the culmination of this game. Indeed, other challenges await this team, in particular with the creation of an AI taken from a horror film. In addition, our teamwork will solve the next problems that we will encounter with gameplay or graphics and others.

We hunt demons together with the Track Your Demon team !
